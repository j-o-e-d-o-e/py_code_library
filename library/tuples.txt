tuples()

data structures

- immutable, very much like strings
- concatenation possible
- extra comma declares one value-expression as a singleton tuple

EXAMPLES
a = ('I', 'am', 'a', 'test', 'tuple')
a[::2]
#prompts: ('I', 'a', 'tuple')

a + ("really",)
#prompts: ('I', 'am', 'a', 'test', 'tuple', 'really')

a[1] #prompts string: 'am'

a[1:2] #prompts tuple: ('am',)

#swap variables:
x = 1
y = 2
(x,y) = (y,x)
