slice

for lists, strings

- [start:end:stride]
- s[i:j] returns a portion of string s from index i to index j-1
- Negative 'stride' goes from right to left, therefore swap 'start' and 'end'

EXAMPLES
"sensible contrasts"[1:8:2]

"hello"[:-1]
#prompts: 'hell'

my_list = range(1, 11)
backwards = my_list[::-1]
print (backwards)
